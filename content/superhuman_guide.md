## THE KEY RECIPES (complete-beginner steps)

### Psyllium husk (your daily miracle)
1 tsp into **300–400 ml plain water** → stir → **drink immediately** (before it thickens) → chase with another glass. Evening, around/after 18:00 (during the fasting evening). Weeks 1–2: 1 tsp · Week 3+: up to 1.5–2 tsp.

### CCF Tea — the gut superpower tea
Lightly crush **½ tsp cumin + ½ tsp coriander seeds + ½ tsp fennel** → boil in **2.5 cups water 5–7 min** → strain → sip warm after lunch. *(Bloated day? swap fennel for ajwain.)*

### Guava-leaf tea
Boil **4–5 tender guava leaves** (from your tree) in 2 cups water 5–8 min → strain → sip warm.

### Moringa / Amla water
**½ tsp powder** in a cup of warm water (amla can be fresh-grated) → drink **fasted, morning**, away from your main meal.

### Daily nut + seed core (start of Meal 1)
**5–6 soaked, peeled almonds + 2 walnut halves + 1 tsp ground flax + 1 tsp soaked chia**, plus **1 tbsp** of the day's seed (pumpkin / sunflower / watermelon, rotating). *Soak almonds + chia the night before.*

### Moong-dal khichdi bowl (Day 1 — the gut-soothing default)
Wash ½ cup foxtail millet (or rice) + ¼ cup moong dal. Temper cumin + hing + ginger in 1 tbsp ghee. Add 1 cup soft veg (carrot, bottle gourd, pumpkin) + turmeric, sauté 2 min. Add millet+dal + 3 cups water + salt → pressure-cook 3 whistles. Mash lightly, top with ghee. **Serve with curd.**

### Idli + mild sambar (fermented cooked)
Steam 3–4 idlis from fermented batter. Serve with warm **mild** sambar (toor dal + soft veg) + a little ghee + **a live ferment side (curd/buttermilk)** to get probiotics.

### Overnight-fermented rice (saddi annam — the star probiotic dinner)
Soak cooked rice in water **overnight**. Next evening: mix with **fresh curd** + a tempering of roasted cumin, hing, curry leaf + pinch salt. Serve cool. *(One of the best gut foods you have — and it's traditional to home.)*

> The full lunch + dinner rotation (8 each) lives in the JSON under `recipes`. They repeat on a cycle so you build kitchen confidence fast.

### What to keep gentle (because of how your gut is right now)
- **Cooked veg only — no raw salads.**
- Onion/garlic: use in tempering and keep moderate; **hing (asafoetida)** is your friend for keeping things calm.
- Ramp psyllium and fermented **gradually**.
- On a tired-gut day, fall back to **khichdi / pongal / soup** — maximum ease.

### Evening magnesium soak (sitz) — 3–4 nights/week
Warm water in a clean tub/basin + **2–3 tbsp Epsom (magnesium) salt** → sit relaxed **12–15 min** → ideally after your evening bowel movement or just before bed → pat dry gently. *(Recovery + calm + magnesium for sleep.)*

### Deep-core / pelvic set (2×/day)
Slow squeeze-and-lift, hold **5s, release 5s × 10**, then **10 quick pulses**. Breathe normally; relax fully between reps. Builds a strong, controlled core base for everything you lift.
